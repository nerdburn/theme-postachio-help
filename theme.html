Getting Started

Thanks for visiting the Postach.io Help Center. If you're new to Postach.io, we recommend you read the FAQ below.

FAQ

How do I create a post?
Simply open up Evernote on your desktop or mobile, create a note in your Postach.io notebook, tag it "published", and then sync Evernote. To learn more about creating posts, check out the Creating Posts section.

How do I add comments to my blog?
We don't support native comments, but you can easily install Disqus comments on any of our themes.

How do I change my portrait photo?
To upload a custom profile photo, create a note in your sites notebook and add the image you'd like to use. Tag the note with "avatar" and sync Evernote. That's it! Pro-tip: square images work best. For more info, check out the How to Change Your Avatar page.
